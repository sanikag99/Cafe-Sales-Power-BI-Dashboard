# ☕ Cafe Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 🔹 Short Description / Purpose

The Cafe Sales Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze cafe sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, and product trends. It helps business owners and managers make data-driven decisions to increase revenue, optimize inventory, and improve overall business performance.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* 📊 Power BI Desktop – Dashboard development and visualization
* 📂 Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation
* 🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Calculations and KPIs
* 🔗 Data Modeling – Relationships between sales and product data
* 📁 File Format – `.pbix` and `.xlsx`

## 📁 Data Source

The dataset contains cafe transaction records, including order details, product categories, sales amount, quantity sold, payment methods, and transaction dates. The data is structured to analyze sales performance, customer preferences, and operational efficiency.

## 🌟 Features / Highlights

### 📌 Business Problem

Cafe businesses often struggle to identify top-performing products, monitor revenue trends, and understand customer purchasing patterns. Manual analysis can be time-consuming and may lead to missed business opportunities.

Key questions addressed:

* Which products generate the highest sales revenue?
* What are the peak sales hours and busiest days?
* Which product categories are most popular?
* How do sales trends change over time?
* Which payment methods are most frequently used?

### 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

To build an interactive analytics tool that:

* Monitors overall sales performance
* Identifies top-selling products and categories
* Tracks revenue trends over time
* Analyzes customer purchasing behavior
* Supports better inventory and business planning

## 📊 Walkthrough of Key Visuals

### 🔢 Key KPIs

* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Total Quantity Sold
* Average Order Value
* Monthly Growth Rate

### ☕ Product Performance Analysis

Shows the best-selling products and identifies items contributing the most revenue.

### 📅 Sales Trend Analysis

Displays daily, weekly, or monthly sales trends to identify growth patterns and seasonal demand.

### 🏷️ Category-wise Sales

Analyzes revenue contribution from different product categories such as Coffee, Tea, Bakery, and Snacks.

### ⏰ Peak Sales Hours

Highlights the busiest hours of the day to support staffing and operational planning.

### 💳 Payment Method Analysis

Shows the distribution of transactions across payment methods such as Cash, Card, and Digital Payments.

### 🎛️ Filters / Slicers

* Product Category
* Product Name
* Date Range
* Payment Method

## 📈 Business Impact & Insights

* Identified top-performing products driving revenue
* Improved inventory planning based on product demand
* Optimized staffing during peak business hours
* Enhanced sales monitoring and decision-making
* Supported revenue growth through data-driven insights
